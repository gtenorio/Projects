About Me

My name is Giovanni Tenorio. I am from San Francisco, California and I am currently a senior at the University of California at Davis. I will be graduating this year with a bachelors in Computer Science. I love learning new, interesting languages and figuring out how to solve tricky problems with them. My main coding experience is with C++ and Python and I also have knowlege of C, Java, SQL, HTML/CSS, and Javascript. If you would like to see some of my past coding work, feel free to browse this site or check out my github.
